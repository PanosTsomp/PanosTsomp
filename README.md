# About Me

Hi! I'm **Panagiotis Ilias Tsompanoglou**, a Digital Systems graduate from the University of Thessaly. 

I enjoy building software, exploring new technologies, and working at the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship.

During my studies I worked on projects in software engineering and machine learning, including my thesis on AI-based disease detection in medical imaging.

I have also been involved in startup and team-based projects, contributing to system design, development workflows, and technical problem solving.

Currently, I’m preparing to start a Master’s degree in Business Information Technology in the Netherlands, while building small projects to refine my skills and experiment with new ideas.
